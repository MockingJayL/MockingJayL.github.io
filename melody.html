Melody Generation

Since the number of songs in the world has been increasing rapidly, new music pieces are in high demand for creativity. However, the innovation from human brains can easily be influenced by the songs they hear every day, but machine learning empowers melody creation with a more random mechanism that unlocks more possibilities in writing new songs. Therefore, I hope to explore how the machine learning approach can be applied in the melody generation process, what is the result of it, and what can be improved in the future. In this project, I use neural networks to train the model with melody data (music 21) and automatically generate a piece of music.

Motivation & Goal

- Create a program to generate melodies to make sure that the songs produced are unique.

- Explore how the machine learning approach can be applied in the melody generation process, what is the result of it, and what can be improved in the future.

Reflection

The final product is indeed really surprising, but the melody generated cannot be considered as a great piece. Because the piece of music is completely created by the program, it lacks emotions and themes. The notes are pretty random, but they seem to follow some kind of order.
