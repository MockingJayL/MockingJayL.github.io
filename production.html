Music Production

This music project is my first "serious" original music-making experiment, including ideation, building chord progression, paving rhythm patterns, creating Motifs to melodies, and finally mixing the whole piece. The song was born at a night when I felt uncertain about the future and a bit afraid of the path I am pursuing, but thinking about what I have done and the people around me, I gained energy again and determineded to walk along this journey bravely. The song's flow basically fits well with my emotional journey, and I tried my best to translate this nuanced emotion into music elements.

Creating Process

Chord Progression

Writing this piece comes with the chord progression first. When I played the keyboard purposelessly at that night, I picked one chord progression I loved. Then I decided to add elements on this chord progression and try to make a whole song based on it.

Rhythm Patterns

Since I want to write in pop genre, I choosed 4/4 Time Signature as the rhythm pattern. Then I tested out some drum machine samples in logic pro to make sure the beats sound "right". I made various beat patterms for verse, chorus, and bridge to create enrich the variation in the song and helps build the emotive experience.

Motif to Melody/Adjust the melody

I started to add melody to the "skeleton" of the music (rhythm and chord progression). I started to find Motif
by looping the chord progression with beats and randomly
playing guitar until something caught my ears. Then I
started recording the melody into the DAW and see how Motif can be adjusted and fit in verse, chorus, and bridge. Subtle adjustments to the melodies also was required by listening many times of what i got so far.

Adjust the whole vibe

Finally, I did mixing for the whole project to make each track combines together. I tried to envision for the song and attempted to make that come to life in a way that the listener can relate to what the visionaries felt as I recorded the song. I used EQ, compress, and tried to make everything sound pristine. During this process, mixing sounds is kind of like cooking, you are balancing all the ingredients(music elements) to make a perfect dish.

Reflection

I love my late night guitar session where I translate my emotion into Motifs. Experiencing the complete production process makes me realize how poweful the DAWs are, and motivates me a lot to learn more recording techniques, plugins' best practices, and the theories behind the hood.

Explore how music and technology can be interweaved together.
